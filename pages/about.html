OUR TEAM

Engineers Without Borders - Cornell is a group of passionate and highly motivated students who aim to create long-lasting solutions to problems in international communities. Members are given the opportunity to nurture their creative and technical expertise by designing and implementing sustainable engineering projects. Through collaboration with communities in developing countries around the world, our members are exposed to diverse cultures while gaining academic experience outside of the classroom environment.

Our growing team of over 40 dedicated students includes undergraduate and graduate students from several of Cornell's colleges including the College of Engineering, the College of Arts and Sciences, and the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences. Our network also extends beyond Cornell's campus to over 250 EWB chapters.

OUR KEY VALUES

GLOBAL AWARNESS

Our international and domestic projects in Tanzania, Bolivia, South Dakota, Tompkins County, and more refelct our diverse experiences.

COMPASSION

Our Engineers Without Borders team works to understand each other and the communities and people we work with.

COLLABORATION

We work closely with both the Cornell Engineering community and the communities we work with to create long-lasting solutions.

TECHNICAL EXPERTISE

Gaining academic experience outside of the classroom, our work incorporates our rigorous engineering training to design and implement our projects in diverse communities.
