DOMESTIC SUBTEAM

ABOUT THE DOMESTIC PROJECT

With housing insecurity and overcrowding as a major problem on the Pine Ridge Reservation in South Dakota, our talented group of engineers from a multitude of backgrounds and majors began the design of a Sustainable Housing Unit, or SHU. Our goal is to produce a sustainable solution for young families by creating a four-person home with all basic utilities and comfortable living space. We are currently engineering the home, assessing cost, and establishing construction documents for future implementation.
